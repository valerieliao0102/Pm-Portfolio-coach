# PM Portfolio Coach 🎯

An AI-powered tool that helps Product Managers build a complete portfolio from their resume — not just summarize it.

## Live Demo

👉 [https://valerieliao0102.github.io/Pm-Portfolio-coach/](https://valerieliao0102.github.io/Pm-Portfolio-coach/)

## What It Does

Most PM portfolios are just resumes in disguise. This tool is different.

1. **Pick a color theme** → Choose from 6 styles
2. **Click the button** → Redirects to Claude.ai with your theme settings pre-loaded
3. **Upload your resume (PDF)** → Claude analyzes your background
4. **Answer questions** → Claude asks one question at a time to draw out the real story behind each project
5. **Get your portfolio** → Complete outline with polished case studies ready to turn into slides

## How to Use

1. Open the [live demo](https://valerieliao0102.github.io/Pm-Portfolio-coach/)
2. Select a color theme
3. Click **開始建立我的作品集 →**
4. You'll be redirected to Claude.ai — upload your PDF resume
5. Claude will guide you through the entire process

## Requirements

- A free [Claude.ai](https://claude.ai) account
- A PDF resume with text content (scanned images won't work)
- That's it — completely free, no API key needed
